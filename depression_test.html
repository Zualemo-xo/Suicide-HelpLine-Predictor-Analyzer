This depression test is based on the nine-item Patient Health Questionnaire (PHQ9), one of the most commonly-used tools used to measure symptoms and severity of depression. A machine learning model is being used to predict the results based on the input obtained.

Completing the test can help you get a better idea of whether you might need help for depression-and suicide as a whole.

Choose one answer for each of the questions below. Be sure to answer all of the questions.

We do not keep a record of the results or any identifying information.
